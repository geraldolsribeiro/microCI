# Pip the Pipeline Ant

<div align="center" markdown="1">

![Pip](images/pip_thumbs_up.png)

</div>

**Pip** is the tireless courier of microCI.

While other automation systems depend on complex infrastructure, **Pip**
carries your build instructions directly to wherever work needs to happen—your
laptop, a build server, a container, or a deployment target. Small, dependable,
and relentlessly practical, **Pip** believes automation should be portable,
auditable, and infrastructure-independent.
